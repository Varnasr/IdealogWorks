**Innovation advisory for development impact, social enterprise, and public interest technology.**

IdealogWorks works at the intersection of ideas, evidence, and execution — helping organisations translate research and strategy into real-world change.

---

## About

IdealogWorks is an innovation and advisory practice focused on three domains:

1. **Development Impact** — Programme strategy, theory of change, and impact measurement for NGOs, foundations, and bilateral agencies
2. **Social Enterprise** — Business model design, financial sustainability, and growth strategy for mission-driven organisations
3. **Public Interest Technology** — Technology strategy, product design, and responsible AI adoption for civic and social organisations

---

## What We Do

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Innovation Advisory** | Structured innovation processes: problem framing, ideation, prototyping, and scale strategy |
| **Impact Strategy** | Theory of change development, MEL framework design, and outcome measurement |
| **Research & Evaluation** | Programme evaluations, formative research, and evidence synthesis |
| **Organisational Design** | Org structure, governance, and team capability building |
| **Technology Strategy** | Product scoping, platform selection, and responsible AI integration |
